Whylaway boarding for cats and dogs

Need to go out of town? Whylaway Boarding Kennel & Cattery can give your pet quality care - and can give you peace of mind. It's our aim to ensure your pets have as much fun during their stay with us as you do on your holiday.

How we started

At Whylaway Boarding Kennels & Cattery we really do care about your pets and we all love what we do. Our staff has over 40 years experience and knowledge in caring for pets. Qualified Vet Nurse on site.

The Location

Whylaway Boarding Kennels & Cattery is situated on 20 acres in a quiet country area, Helidon Spa. Offering modern and professional setup we warmly invite inspections, please take a look at our gallery.
